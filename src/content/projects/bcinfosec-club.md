# Bellevue College InfoSec Club Website

The official website for the Bellevue College Information Security Club, designed to serve as a central hub for club members and prospective students interested in cybersecurity.

## Purpose

This website serves multiple purposes:
- **Information Hub**: Provides details about club activities, meetings, and events
- **Resource Center**: Offers cybersecurity learning resources and links
- **Community Building**: Helps connect students with shared interests in information security
- **Event Promotion**: Showcases upcoming workshops, competitions, and guest speakers

## Technical Details

Built with Hugo, a fast static site generator:
- **Static Site Generation**: Ensures fast loading times and easy hosting
- **Markdown Content**: Easy content management for club officers
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
- **SEO Optimized**: Proper meta tags and structured data for search visibility

## Impact

The website has helped increase club membership and engagement by providing a professional online presence and making it easier for students to find information about cybersecurity opportunities at Bellevue College.
